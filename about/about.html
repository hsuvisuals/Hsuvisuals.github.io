Who Am I?

As a Digital Media Artist, I produce websites, videos and photos that come together to tell both a unique story and evoke emotions. Using visual means, I want to explore the infinite possibilities that digital media offers. My work serves to visually express these stories and emotions that appeal to my viewers. Each piece forces viewers to confront themselves with their own ideas, thoughts, and emotions, both negative and positive. I believe that all people go through different yet similar experiences, and my art captures those experiences and help people to reflect and grow. I use a combination of music and visuals to encapsulate these stories and help me inspire others to draw their own conclusion. Technology is constantly evolving, and I want to combine these new techniques and methods to challenge the traditional idea of what art is.
